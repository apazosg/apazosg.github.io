Privacy Policy for PixPocket

Last updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application PixPocket, developed by Adrián Garrido Pazos. PixPocket is a retro-style camera application that allows users to pixelate photos and create unique GIFs.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information directly. All photo processing and GIF creation are performed locally on your device. Your images are never uploaded to our servers.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Services

Our application may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve relevant ads, Google AdMob may collect and use certain data, including:

  • Device advertising identifiers.
  • IP address.
  • General usage and app performance data.

For more information on how Google manages your data, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps

3. Camera and Storage Permissions

PixPocket requires access to your device's camera and photo gallery to function correctly. These permissions are used exclusively to allow you to take photos, apply retro effects, and save the final results to your device's storage.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any personal data with third parties, except for the requirements of the advertising services mentioned above.
